A look at how we are building a research lab at the intersection of AI, computer graphics, and human-computer interaction in Tokyo.

Shanda AI Research Tokyo was founded with a clear mission: to develop the next generation of interactive digital humans. Our team brings together expertise from computer vision, natural language processing, speech synthesis, motion generation, and neural rendering.

Working closely with collaborators from **The University of Tokyo** and **Institute of Science Tokyo**, we are tackling fundamental challenges that span the full stack of digital human intelligence — from low-level rendering and animation to high-level reasoning and personality modeling.

Stay tuned for more updates as we share our research with the community.
