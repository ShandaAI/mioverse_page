**MioVerse.ai** is a research platform by Shanda Tokyo Research Institute, bridging **Digital Humans** and **Virtual Worlds** through multimodal AI.

Our work is organized around two complementary pillars:

**M.I.O Live** (Multimodal Interactive Omni-Avatar) — An end-to-end framework for autonomous digital humans that think, speak, emote, gesture, and render in real-time. Five cascading modules enable personality-consistent expression, adaptive interaction, and continuous self-evolution at 30fps.

**ALAYA STUDIO** — A research team focuses on AI-native games-related techniques, such as world model, generative rendering, AI agent, and AI NPC. We aim to build a generative game engine and further develop the next-generation games. Your imagination is the engine. Interaction creates the game.

Together, these projects form a unified vision: intelligent digital humans inhabiting richly interactive AI-generated worlds.
