**MioVerse.ai** is a research platform by Shanda Tokyo Research Institute, bridging **Digital Humans** and **Virtual Worlds** through multimodal AI.

Our work is organized around two complementary pillars:

**Project M.I.O** (Multimodal Interactive Omni-Avatar) — An end-to-end framework for autonomous digital humans that think, speak, emote, gesture, and render in real-time. Five cascading modules enable personality-consistent expression, adaptive interaction, and continuous self-evolution at 30fps.

**Project Sekai** (Interactive World Model) — A DiT-based generation system that creates explorable virtual worlds from text or image input, with real-time 24fps navigation, 10s+ coherent context windows, and dynamic event editing mid-exploration.

Together, these projects form a unified vision: intelligent digital humans inhabiting richly interactive AI-generated worlds.
